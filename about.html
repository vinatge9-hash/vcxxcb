Our Story

Brew & Beans started as a simple idea: create a neighborhood coffee shop where excellent coffee meets a warm, welcoming space. Our founders traveled to coffee-growing regions, learning about terroir, roasting techniques, and the people behind every bean. Today, we roast in-house, celebrate small-batch craft, and bring that same care to every cup we serve. We are committed to sustainable sourcing, fair labor practices, and building relationships with farmers who share our values. Our team loves sharing stories about beans, roasting profiles, and the craft of coffee with every guest who walks through our doors.

Beyond great coffee, Brew & Beans is about community. We host weekly tastings, workshop events, and partner with local bakers to offer fresh pastries that pair perfectly with our brews. Whether you’re grabbing a quick espresso to-go or settling in for a slow afternoon with a book, our goal is to create a space where people feel at home.

We’re grateful for the feedback from our guests, and we’re always evolving—introducing new single-origin pour-overs, seasonal pastries, and occasional guest roasters. Thank you for supporting a local, independent coffee shop that treats every cup as a craft and every guest as family.

Sourcing

We partner with farms that emphasize sustainability, fair wages, and transparent farming practices. Our beans are roasted in-house to preserve flavor and aroma.

Roasting

Small-batch roasting allows us to highlight origin flavors and maintain consistency across cups.

Community
